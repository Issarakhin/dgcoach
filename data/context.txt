 Comprehensive Customer Support Process
-----------------------------------

Initial Contact:
When a customer first contacts us, their query is logged into our customer support system. This ensures that no query is overlooked and allows us to track the resolution process.

Query Categorization:
The query is categorized based on its nature – general information, course-related inquiries, payment issues, technical support, etc. This helps us assign the right personnel to address the issue promptly.

Acknowledgment:
An acknowledgment email or message is sent to the customer, confirming that we have received their query and are working on it. This email includes an estimated response time and any immediate steps the customer can take.

Detailed Response:
Our support team investigates the query and provides a detailed response. If the issue requires more time, we keep the customer informed about the progress.

Resolution and Follow-Up:
Once the issue is resolved, we send a follow-up message to ensure the customer is satisfied with the resolution. We also encourage feedback to improve our services.

Documentation:
All interactions are documented for future reference. This helps us in analyzing common issues and improving our support processes.
